About School

The school was founded on 21 January 1991. Gradually over the years, we ramped up classes till Std X. The “breaking ground” ceremony for Mirjanhat campus was done in 2000, and we inaugurated Mirjanhat campus in January 2004 with a 3-storied block housing classes till Std. X. We started with a complete infrastructure consisting of laboratories, computer rooms, activity rooms and playground. The foundation stone for second block was laid in May 2009. By 2016, we had established a second block with three floors having 30 additional classrooms, 3 activity rooms, laboratories, and a fully functional auditorium. As of now, the school has 75+ classrooms, 3 computer labs, 4 science laboratories and separate rooms for music, indoor sports, extra-curricular activities. The school has provision for proper hygiene and sanitation facilities along with an expansive 5-acre playground.
